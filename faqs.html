Frequently Asked Questions

What types of photography services do you offer?

We offer a wide range of photography services including portrait, event, landscape, travel, commercial, product, and food photography. We also provide customized photo editing and retouching services.

How can I book a photoshoot?

You can book a photoshoot by contacting us through our Contact Us page. Fill out the form with your details and preferred date, and we will get back to you to confirm the booking.

What is your pricing for different photography services?

Our pricing varies depending on the type and scope of the project. Please reach out to us via our Contact Us page for a customized quote based on your specific needs.

Do you offer photo editing and retouching services?

Yes, we offer professional photo editing and retouching services to enhance your images and ensure they look their best.

Can I get digital copies of my photos?

Absolutely! We provide high-resolution digital copies of all the photos from your session. You can download them from a private online gallery that we will set up for you.

How long does it take to receive the final photos?

The turnaround time for the final photos depends on the nature and volume of the project. Typically, you can expect to receive your edited photos within 2-3 weeks after the photoshoot.

Do you travel for photoshoots?

Yes, we are available for travel and destination photoshoots. Additional travel fees may apply depending on the location. Please contact us for more details.

What is your cancellation policy?

If you need to cancel or reschedule your photoshoot, please notify us at least 48 hours in advance. Cancellations made within 48 hours of the scheduled session may be subject to a cancellation fee.

Do you offer printed photo albums or prints?

Yes, we offer a variety of printed products including photo albums, prints, canvases, and more. Let us know your preferences and we can provide you with options and pricing.
